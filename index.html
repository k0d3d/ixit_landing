Register and Login

Once you have iXit installed on your android device, registering for an account is easy. You can login using your registered email address or phone number.

Pick your uploads

You can upload files by launching the app and selecting 'choose files' on the upload tab. You can also upload files from your file manager and gallery android application.

Share files or download later

Once a file is successfully uploaded, a unique link is created for the file. You can share this link to anyone with or witout an iXit account. Use the share button to attach links in emails, sms and chats.
